Course Introduction

Maize remains one of the most common crops in Nigeria. It grows well in almost every state in Nigeria and produces high yields making it one of the most profitable farming ventures. Because maize is used for both human and animal feed, and is still useful for industrial purposes, any farmer investing in maize farming should expect to make a respectable earning. However, things must be done properly to guarantee maximum yield.
