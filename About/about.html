About Us

Located 15 miles south of the historic Boston Fish Pier, we chose the Hingham Shipyard for our North American headquarters because of the unique parallels drawn between the small fishing villages of Norway and the rich fishing history of the coast of Massachusetts. Since the early 1900s the Boston Fish Pier has been an essential hub for the North American seafood supply and remains to this day a hub for fishery activities. The Boston Fish Pier and many other northeast hubs supply seafood throughout New England and beyond. New England seafood traditions inspire and influence culinary practices throughout North America. We are proud to be a part of this vibrant seafood landscape rooted in historic tradition and propelled forward by innovative efforts in sustainability. Our Norwegian perspective is enriched by the New England mentality and this married approach allows us to add incredible value to the seafood landscape of North America. From Norwegian fjords to our North American customers, we remain connected by sustainable seafood across oceans. That is the Villa Way.
